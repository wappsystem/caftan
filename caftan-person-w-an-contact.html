Thank you for your interest in this important new research study.

You are invited to participate in CAFTAN, a research study investigating a plant-based treatment for Anorexia Nervosa.

You may be eligible if you:

  1. Have anorexia nervosa
  2. Are aged 12-50 years old
  3. Live in NSW
  4. You are currently undergoing therapy
  5. Have a doctor that takes care of you
